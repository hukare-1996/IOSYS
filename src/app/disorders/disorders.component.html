PERINATAL MOOD AND ANXIETY DISORDERS

THE MAIN DIAGNOSES

The term Perinatal Mood and Anxiety Disorders is used to encompass Depression in the pre-partum (before), antepartum

(during) and postpartum (after) periods and affects approximately 1 in 7 women for each pregnancy experience. While

"Postpartum Depression" is the most known, "postpartum" in fact means "after pregnancy."
